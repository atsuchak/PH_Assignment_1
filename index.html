Frequently Asked Questions

What is the refund policy?

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another attendee.

Will there be virtual attendance options?

Yes! We will live-stream all keynote sessions and select technical tracks. Virtual-only passes will be available closer to the event date.

Are meals included with the ticket?

Both Standard and Pro tickets include networking lunches and coffee breaks. Pro tickets also include exclusive access to the VIP networking dinner.

Is there a code of conduct?

Absolutely. We are committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment for all attendees. Our full Code of Conduct is strictly enforced.
